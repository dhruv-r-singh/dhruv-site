SWARM Systems
Active
Every drone in SWARM is identical hardware. What makes them different is what they're told to do and that changes in real time based on what the swarm actually needs. No central brain, no single point of failure. One node goes down, the rest pick up the slack and keep going. This one's still in progress.

AdaptFlow
Complete
A greywater management system that recycles household wastewater using sensors and AI to measure water quality and direct it where it can be safely reused. Selected as one of 23 teams globally for the MIT Regenerative Futures Challenge. Presented at the MIT Museum to over 500 people.

04
Flowing Water Generator
Active
A low-cost fluvial kinetic energy harvester built for under $15. Uses an Archimedes screw turbine to convert flowing water into electricity via a DC generator, designed for off-grid homes, refugee camps, and emergency disaster zones.
2024 Complete
